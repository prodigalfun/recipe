The Pineapple Pizza Recipe

Background

Pineapple pizza is widely hated, but I'm here to tell you the hate is unjustified. Why? Because pineapple pizza is frankly
to put it simply, awesome. And this recipe is here to prove it to you. Simple, elegant, and cultured, this recipe has it all. It's
filling, wonderful and a joy to consume. It's definitely more time consuming than the other recipes on this site, but
well worth it in the end.

Ingredients

1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon cream of tartar

½ teaspoon baking soda

¼ teaspoon salt

½ cup butter, softened

1 large egg

1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese

3 pineapple slices, exact size can vary

Directions

.Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

.Make the cookie crust: Combine flour, cream of tartar, baking soda, and salt in a medium bowl and set aside.

Cream together butter and 3/4 cup sugar until smooth in a large bowl. Add in egg and beat well.

Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, 8 to 10 minutes. Cool.

Prepare the filling: Beat cream cheese with remaining 1/2 cup sugar and vanilla in a large bowl until light and fluffy.
Spread evenly over the top of the cooled crust.

.Arrange blueberries, strawberries, and banana on top of the filling, and chill.
